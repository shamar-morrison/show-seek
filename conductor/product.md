# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
ShowSeek is a modern, feature-rich mobile application built with React Native and Expo that allows users to discover, search, and track movies and TV shows. Powered by The Movie Database (TMDB) API and Firebase, ShowSeek provides a seamless experience for entertainment enthusiasts to manage their watchlists, track episode progress, rate content, and receive release notifications.

## Target Audience
ShowSeek is designed for a broad spectrum of entertainment enthusiasts:
- **Casual Viewers:** Users looking for quick recommendations and a way to discover trending content.
- **Power Users:** Hardcore cinephiles and binge-watchers who require detailed episode tracking, rating history, and comprehensive library management.
- **Organizers:** Individuals who want a centralized hub to organize their watchlists and viewing history across various streaming platforms.

## Primary Goals
The core objectives of ShowSeek are:
- **Comprehensive Tracking:** Providing a one-stop solution for tracking progress across both movies and TV series, including episode-level granularity.
- **Premium Experience:** Delivering a high-performance, seamless UI/UX that mirrors the quality of top-tier native streaming applications.
- **Unified Ecosystem:** Integrating deeply with external services like TMDB for metadata and Trakt for history/rating synchronization to ensure a consistent experience.

## Design Philosophy
- **Minimalism:** Prioritizing a clean, focused interface where content remains the hero. The design reduces clutter to ensure that information is easily digestible while maintaining a modern aesthetic.

## Future Priorities
- **Social Connectivity:** Expanding the platform to include social features, such as sharing lists with others, following friends to see their activity, and participating in community-driven ratings and discussions.
